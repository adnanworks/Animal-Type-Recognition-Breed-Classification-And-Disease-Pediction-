Current Limitations of Animal Identification

Travellers often rely on visual estimation, prior knowledge, or web searches. These methods are inaccurate, slow, and fail to identify animal breeds. Traditional guides and printed resources are region-limited and provide no real-time recognition. Existing apps often lack safety alerts and breed-level detail.
